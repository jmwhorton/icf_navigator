About the results from the study

Will you tell me the results of the study?

We will not send you the overall results of the study or your own results.

Once the study is done, we will send you:

  • A summary of all the results from the entire study
  • Your individual results

We will not send you:

  • A summary of all the results from the entire study
  • Your individual results

Will you tell me anything you learn that may affect my health?

Since this study is for research purposes only, we will not tell you if we learn anything that might impact your health.

[Explanation to 15.3, what details will be shared and how]

After we tell you the genetic results, you may be upset by learning this information about you or your family. For example, this can happen if you learn about a genetic disorder that you did not know about yet. If you are concerned about the results, share your concerns about the results with your regular doctor.

Will you share study results with the public?

We may share overall results in papers or public talks. These will not include your personal results or any details that would identify you.
